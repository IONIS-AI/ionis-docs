# Research

Technical papers documenting the IONIS project's methodology, infrastructure, and lessons learned.

## Papers

### [The Dialectical Engine](the-dialectical-engine.md)
*Multi-Agent Falsification in Scientific Software Development*

How five AI agents and a human domain expert used dialectical analysis to debug a neural network — and discovered that the failure record was more valuable than the model.

---

### [From Nanometers to Neurons](from-nanometers-to-neurons.md)
*Applying Semiconductor Process Control to Machine Learning*

CLCA/8D methodology from semiconductor manufacturing applied to neural network development. Eight architectural dead ends, each with a documented root cause.

---

### [Sovereign Infrastructure for Citizen Science](sovereign-infrastructure.md)
*Building a Self-Hosted Research Lab for Radio Propagation Analysis*

How a self-hosted lab processes 14.68 billion rows on commodity hardware — 24.67 Mrps ingestion, 60 GB/s query throughput, 30 tok/s local LLM inference — at 1.3% of equivalent cloud cost.

### [Who Says Hermits Don't Have Friends](who-says-hermits-dont-have-friends.md)
*The Human Story Behind a Sovereign AI Lab*

The non-technical story of building IONIS. Five AI agents, a 96 GB GPU, a Thunderbolt cable, and a callsign. The hermit has more collaborators than most startups.
